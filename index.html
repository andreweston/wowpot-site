Wheel of Wishes

Wheel of Wishes was the first modern WowPot release (2020) and remains the flagship title tied most closely to the progressive network. It is designed around bonus-trigger gameplay rather than base-game payouts, making it one of the most jackpot-focused titles in the entire series.

  • Provider: Microgaming / Alchemy Gaming
  • Year of Release: 2020
  • RTP: ~93.34%
  • Volatility: Low–Medium
  • Best For: Casual Players
Full Game Details

Wheel of Wishes is a 5-reel, 10-payline slot built almost entirely around its progressive jackpot mechanics. While the base game includes traditional paylines and symbol combinations, the primary objective is triggering the Power Spin and jackpot wheel features.

Symbols

High-value symbols include the genie lamp, treasure chest, mystical book, potion vial, and jewel amulet. Lower-value symbols consist of traditional card ranks. The Wild substitutes for all regular symbols and pays up to 50x stake for five-of-a-kind combinations. The Wheel of Wishes symbol acts as both scatter and bonus trigger.

Power Spin Feature

The main mechanic is the Power Spin, triggered when two or more Wheel symbols land anywhere on the reels. During this feature, reels 1 and 5 transform into matching high-value symbols while the central reels merge into a giant 3×3 reel showing oversized symbols, increasing payout potential significantly.

Jackpot Trigger

The progressive jackpot bonus activates when the Bonus Wheel symbol fully covers the central reels during a Power Spin. Players then spin a wheel that guarantees one of four jackpots: Mini, Minor, Major, or WowPot.

Power Mode

An optional Power Mode increases the stake by 10× but ensures every spin is a Power Spin, significantly increasing jackpot-trigger chances.

Overall, Wheel of Wishes is built for players targeting jackpots rather than steady wins, making timing and session management critical.

Book of Atem WowPot

Book of Atem WowPot combines the classic expanding-symbol book mechanic with the shared WowPot progressive system. It is one of the most balanced entries in the network, blending structured bonus play with jackpot potential.

  • Provider: Microgaming / Games Global
  • Year of Release: 2020
  • RTP: ~93.5%
  • Volatility: High
  • Best For: VIP Players
Full Game Details

Book of Atem uses a traditional 5-reel, 10-payline layout and closely follows the classic “Book” slot formula while layering in progressive mechanics.

Symbols

High-value symbols include Pharaoh characters and themed Egyptian icons, while lower-value symbols consist of traditional card ranks. The Book of Atem symbol functions as both Wild and Scatter, substituting for all symbols and triggering bonus features.

Free Spins Feature

Landing three or more Book symbols awards 10 free spins and selects one expanding symbol at random. That symbol expands across reels during free spins, creating strong win potential. Retriggers are possible with additional scatters.

Jackpot Trigger

The WowPot jackpot feature can activate randomly after collecting Book symbols during regular play or bonus rounds. Once triggered, players spin the jackpot wheel and are guaranteed one of the four progressive prizes.

Because of its higher variance and feature dependency, Book of Atem appeals strongly to experienced and high-stakes players.
